Privacy Policy

Astitva Social Welfare Foundation (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit https://astitva.org.in/ and interact with our digital platforms, including Facebook and Google Ads.

By using our website, you consent to the terms outlined below:

  1. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following types of information:


    1. 1.1 Personal Information

      When you fill out a form, subscribe to updates, donate, or interact with our ads, we may collect:

      • • Name
      • • Email address
      • • Phone number
      • • Address
      • • Payment information (for donations)

    2. 1.2 Non-Personal Information

      We also collect data that does not directly identify you, including:

      • • Browser type and version
      • • IP address
      • • Device type
      • • Pages visited and time spent on the website
      • • Click behavior on ads

    3. 1.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

      We use cookies, pixels, and tracking technologies to improve user experience and analyze engagement with our website and ads. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.



  2. How We Use Your Information

    We use collected data for the following purposes:


    • • To improve user experience on our website.
    • • To communicate with you regarding events, donations, or volunteer opportunities.
    • • To process donations securely.
    • • For marketing and retargeting on platforms like Facebook, Google Ads, and Instagram.
    • • To analyze campaign performance and improve our outreach.


  3. Sharing of Information

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. However, we may share it with:


    • • Third-party service providers (e.g., payment processors, email services) to support our operations.
    • • Advertising platforms (Google Ads, Facebook Ads) for remarketing campaigns.
    • • Legal authorities if required by law.

      • We ensure all third parties comply with data privacy laws and use secure measures to protect your information.



  4. Facebook and Google Ads Compliance

    To comply with Facebook & Google Ads policies, we:


    • • Use Facebook Pixel & Google Analytics for tracking and remarketing.
    • • Do not collect sensitive data (e.g., religious beliefs, political opinions).
    • • Provide transparent opt-out options for ad targeting.
    • • Follow data protection standards to prevent misuse of information.

    Ad Preferences: You can manage your ad preferences via:

    • • Google Ads Settings: https://adssettings.google.com/
    • • Facebook Ad Preferences: https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences


  5. Data Security

    We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online system is 100% secure. If you suspect any security issues, please contact us immediately.



  6. Your Rights & Choices

    You have the right to:


    • • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
    • • Opt out of marketing emails by clicking “unsubscribe” in our emails.
    • • Disable cookies and tracking through browser settings
    • • Follow data protection standards to prevent misuse of information.

    To request data access or deletion, email us at info@astitva.org.in



  7. Third-Party Links

    Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before sharing any information.



  8. Children’s Privacy

    We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover such data, we delete it immediately.



  9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

    We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. Continued use of our website means acceptance of the updated policy

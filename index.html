About Astitva

We live in a country where most of its population resides in rural areas. And without rural resurgence, no true growth can take place. By empowering communities and providing a conducive environment that enriches all, we aim to uplift the nation. With unique programs crafted for every individual, Astitva seeks to transform every sphere of its life, thus fulfilling the intention of its existence.

Astitva is a story of a significant socio-economic revolution that empowers communities from situations of unemployment, illiteracy, health issues, and social injustice by transforming their surroundings into booming industries, education, and a promise of limitless growth.

A Tryst with Change
Our journey to becoming beacons of change began years ago.

With a passion for solving problems and empowering the residents of Shirpur in the Dhule district of Maharashtra, we set up Astitva. Our organisation consists of enthusiastic humanitarians and activists committed to creating lasting and sustainable change across communities, the environment, and within ourselves.
